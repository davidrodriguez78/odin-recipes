Orange Frosty

Description

This copycat orange dreamsicle frosty, dreamy, creamy, and sweet, is best made in small quantities—no brain freeze!—but you can double it for a larger serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup freshly-squeezed orange juice
  • 1 cup whipped cream or whipped topping
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Pour orange juice into ice cube trays and freeze until solid, about 3 hours.

    2. Step 2

  2. Place orange ice cubes, whipped cream, sweetened condensed milk, and vanilla in a blender and blend until smooth, thick, and creamy. Serve immediately.
